The Scarlet Witch

The woman who ended the entire mutant race with a single sentence.

Here is a time-line of the Scarlet Witch's life:

  • 1989 - Born in Sokovia, Europe
  • 1999 - Wanda's parents were killed in a a USA bombing using Stark tech, due to Wanda's latent abilities, she was able to save herself and her brother Pietro.
  • 2005 - Due to increased tensions caused by the US Military presence in Sokovia, Wanda and Pietro became politically active through protests and were subsequently, recruited and radicalized by Hydra where they underwent strange experiments. When exposed to Loki's sceptre (the mind stone) Wanda's latent powers were unlocked whilst her brother developed super speed.
  • 2007 - Wanda and her brother fight the Avengers ini defence of Hydra and fail their mission. They later join forces with Ultron and severely weaken the Avengers
  • 2008 - Wanda and her brother grow weary of Ultrons plan and eventually join the Avengers to stand against him. Ultron is defeated, however Pietro is killed,leaving Wanda devastated. Wanda is comforted by vision and they begin their bond.
